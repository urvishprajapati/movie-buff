Featured Movies

Movie-1: KGF

In the film KGF, Rocky, a high-ranking mercenary working for a prominent gold mafia in Bombay, seeks power and wealth to fulfill a promise made to his mother. Due to his growing fame, the mafia leaders hire him to assassinate Garuda, the ruthless son of the founder of Kolar Gold Fields.

Movie-2: SINGHAM

Bajirao Singham (Ajay Devgn) is an honest police sub-inspector in the small town of Shivgadh, Maharashtra. He fights against corruption and is transferred to Goa after a clash with a goon. In Goa, Singham faces off against a corrupt politician and gangster, Jaykant Shikre (Prakash Raj), who has powerful contacts in the police.
